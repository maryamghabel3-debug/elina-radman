## 🔍 WEEKLY ANALYSIS QUESTIONS

Every Sunday, answer these:

1. **Which video performed best?** Why? (Hook? Trend? Topic? Time posted?)
2. **Which video flopped?** Why? (Hook weak? Wrong audio? Topic boring?)
3. **Which content pillar got the most engagement?** → Double down next week
4. **What time did my best video get posted?** → Post at that time consistently
5. **What comments did I get?** → Ideas for future content
6. **Which hashtags brought the most views?** → Refine hashtag strategy
7. **Did I post every day?** → If not, why? Fix the bottleneck.

## 🤝 ENGAGEMENT PROTOCOL

### Daily Engagement Routine (30 min, split into 3 sessions)

**Session 1: Before posting (10 min)**
- Comment on 10 posts in #PetiteFashion
- Reply to 3-5 Stories from creators in your niche (500-5000 followers)
- "Love this outfit! Where's the blazer from?" (genuine, specific)

**Session 2: After posting (10 min)**
- Reply to every single comment on YOUR post
- Heart reactions, genuine replies, ask follow-up questions
- This signals the algorithm: "high engagement content"

**Session 3: Evening (10 min)**
- DM 3-5 new followers: "Hey! Thanks for following. Love your style 🤍"
- Engage with 10 posts from your niche
- Comment on 5 posts using your branded hashtag #StyledByElina

### Comment Templates (Personalize These!)
```
"Love how you styled those trousers! Are they petite-friendly? 👀"
"This outfit is so elegant. The color palette is perfection ✨"
"Where is this bag from? I've been looking for something exactly like this!"
"As a fellow petite, I appreciate this content SO much. Following! 🤍"
```

## 🚨 RED FLAGS TO WATCH

| Problem | Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Low views (<200) | Weak hook, wrong audio, wrong time | Test new hooks, use trending sounds, check best posting time |
| High views, low engagement | No CTA, boring content | Add clear CTA, ask questions, make it interactive |
| Low watch time | Video too long, boring middle | Cut to 15-20 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds |
| Losing followers | Inconsistent posting, off-brand content | Stick to pillars, post daily, same aesthetic |
| No new followers | No discovery, weak hashtags | Use trending audio, better hashtags, more Duets/Stitches |

## 📈 GROWTH ROADMAP

### Month 1: Foundation
- **Goal:** 500-1000 TikTok followers
- **Focus:** DAILY posting, finding your style, learning what works
- **Key metric:** Which content type gets best watch time?
- **Don't worry about:** Going viral, brand deals, perfect editing

### Month 2: Momentum
- **Goal:** 1K-5K TikTok followers
- **Focus:** Double down on winning content types, first collabs
- **Key metric:** Follower conversion rate (views → follows)
- **Start:** 1 Duet/Stitch per week, 1 LIVE session

### Month 3: Acceleration
- **Goal:** 5K-10K TikTok followers
- **Focus:** Signature series, brand pitching, community building
- **Key metric:** Engagement rate (likes + comments / views)
- **Start:** Pitch 10 micro-brands for gifted collaborations

### Month 4-6: Monetization
- **Goal:** 10K-50K TikTok followers
- **Focus:** Paid brand deals, affiliate marketing, digital products
- **Key metric:** Revenue per post
- **Launch:** First digital product (style guide / e-book)

## 🎤 LIVE SESSION PLAN (Start Week 3)

**When:** 1x per week, 30 minutes
**Platform:** TikTok LIVE
**Format:** "Styling Q&A with Elina"

**Structure:**
```
0-5 min:    Welcome, show today's outfit, chat casually
5-15 min:   Answer audience questions (collected via Stories before)
15-25 min:   "Style This" — audience sends dilemmas, you solve them
25-30 min:   Wrap up, tease next week's topic, thank viewers
```

**Topics to rotate:**
- "Style My Body Type" — solve real follower dilemmas
- "Wardrobe Audit" — review a follower's closet live
- "Shopping Haul Review" — show recent purchases
- "Trend Report" — what's in, what's out this season
