# Character Consistency Guide

## Objective
Maintain a 100% consistent face, body type, and overall aesthetic for the AI Influencer (Elina Radman) across all generated photos and videos.

## Core Technologies
1. **ComfyUI**: The central workflow engine.
2. **InstantID**: For robust zero-shot face identity preservation.
3. **HunyuanVideo**: For high-quality, consistent video generation.

## Best Practices
- **Reference Images**: Always use the core set of 5 approved reference images of Elina.
- **Prompt Engineering**: Ensure physical descriptions (hair color, eye shape) are consistently included in negative and positive prompts.
- **Lighting & Angles**: Standardize the lighting setups in prompts to avoid weird shadows distorting facial features.
