# Platform Strategies for Elina Radman

## Instagram
- **Focus**: High-quality lifestyle and tech aesthetics.
- **Content Types**: Reels, Carousels, and Stories.
- **Posting Frequency**: 1 post per day, 3-5 stories per day.
- **Hashtags**: #ElinaRadman #AIInfluencer #TechLife #FutureOfAI

## TikTok
- **Focus**: Engaging, fast-paced trends and educational tech snippets.
- **Content Types**: 15-60 second vertical videos.
- **Posting Frequency**: 1-2 videos per day.
- **Style**: Humorous, relatable, highly visual.

## YouTube
- **Focus**: In-depth tech tutorials, behind-the-scenes, and AI discussions.
- **Content Types**: Long-form videos (8-15 mins) and Shorts.
- **Posting Frequency**: 1 long-form video per week, 3 Shorts per week.

## Pinterest
- **Focus**: Inspiration boards, aesthetic tech setups, and AI art.
- **Content Types**: Static images, infographics, and short idea pins.
- **Posting Frequency**: 3-5 pins per day.
