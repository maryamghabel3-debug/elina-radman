## SCRIPT 8: "Quiet Luxury On A Budget" (Week 2)

```
[HOOK - 0:00 to 0:03]
"Want to look expensive but your budget says otherwise?
Here's how."

[0:03 to 0:10]
"First: Fabric over brand.
A polyester top from a luxury brand
still looks like polyester.
Natural fibers: cotton, linen, wool, viscose.
These drape better. These photograph better. These LAST."

[0:10 to 0:17]
"Second: Color palette is free.
Stick to neutrals: cream, beige, black, charcoal, camel.
They always look more expensive than bright colors.
And everything matches. Less clothes, more outfits."

[0:17 to 0:25]
"Third: Details matter.
Iron your clothes. Remove pills from knits.
Polish your shoes. Trim loose threads.
These things cost zero dollars and make everything look 10x better."

[CTA - 0:25 to 0:30]
"What's your best 'look expensive on a budget' tip?
Share in the comments — I want to learn from you too 💫
#QuietLuxury #BudgetStyle #StyleTips #LookExpensiveForLess"
```
