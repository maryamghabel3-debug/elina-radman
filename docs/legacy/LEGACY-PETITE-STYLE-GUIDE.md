## 👔 THE 10 PETITE COMMANDMENTS

### 1. High-Waisted Everything
- Low-rise cuts your body at the widest part of your hip
- High-waist starts at your natural waist (smallest part) = elongates legs
- **Ideal rise:** 10-12 inches for your frame

### 2. Cropped Lengths ONLY
- Full-length pants on you = puddling at the floor
- **Ideal inseam:** 24-26 inches (not the standard 30-32)
- Cropped = intentional. Too long = looks like they don't fit.

### 3. Monochrome Magic
- Same color top + bottom = no visual break = taller silhouette
- Doesn't have to be same shade. Cream + beige + camel all count.
- Dark monochrome (all charcoal) = slimming + elongating

### 4. Vertical Lines
- V-necks elongate the neck
- Vertical stripes (never horizontal!)
- Long necklaces (20-24 inches)
- Front crease on trousers
- Unbuttoned jackets create vertical line

### 5. Small-Scale Everything
- Small bags (micro to mini, nothing oversized)
- Thin belts (1-2cm, not chunky)
- Delicate jewelry (not statement pieces that overwhelm)
- Small prints (not large florals or bold graphics)

### 6. Fitted Shoulders
- Most important fit point for petites
- If shoulders droop, you look like a child in adults' clothes
- The shoulder seam should sit exactly at your shoulder bone
- Tailor can fix this on MOST garments

### 7. Nude Shoes = Endless Legs
- Nude/beige/cream shoes that match your skin tone
- Pointed toe > round toe (adds visual length)
- Low vamp (shows more foot = more leg)
- Avoid ankle straps (cuts the leg line)

### 8. Tailor Everything
- Budget 10-15% of every clothing purchase for alterations
- Hem pants, shorten sleeves, take in waists, crop jackets
- A $50 tailored blazer > $500 untailored blazer
- Find ONE good tailor. Build a relationship. Tip well.

### 9. Proportions Over Trends
- Rule of thirds: 1/3 top, 2/3 bottom = ideal proportion
- Tuck your top in (front tuck, full tuck, belt)
- Cropped jackets hit at hip bone, not mid-thigh
- Midi dresses hit just above ankle (not mid-calf, not floor-length)

### 10. Confidence Is The Cheat Code
- The most expensive thing you can wear is posture
- Shoulders back. Chin parallel to floor.
- Walk like you're 5'9" — people perceive what you project
- Never apologize for your height. Frame it as a feature, not a bug.

## 🎨 QUICK STYLE RECIPES

### Recipe 1: "Taller In 30 Seconds"
- High-waisted black trousers
- Cream knit tucked in
- Nude pointed flats
- Long gold necklace
- No belt (monochrome line)
→ **Result:** One unbroken vertical line from neck to toe

### Recipe 2: "Instant Polish"
- Camel cropped blazer (fitted shoulders)
- White tee (tucked)
- Dark straight jeans (cropped length)
- Black pointed pumps
- Small structured bag
→ **Result:** French-girl effortless chic, petite-scaled

### Recipe 3: "Date Night Elegance"
- Midi slip dress (hits above ankle)
- Cropped trench coat over shoulders
- Strappy nude heels
- Delicate gold jewelry
- Small clutch
→ **Result:** Understated luxury, perfectly proportioned

### Recipe 4: "Weekend Cool"
- Cream wide-leg jeans (cropped)
- Charcoal fine knit (tucked)
- Camel blazer (optional)
- White slim sneakers
- Crossbody bag
→ **Result:** Relaxed but intentional
